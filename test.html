Intelligent Text Highlighting & Organization

Amber revolutionizes the way you interact with web content by providing a powerful yet intuitive text highlighting system. Unlike traditional bookmarking tools that save entire pages, Amber allows you to capture and preserve the exact passages that matter most to you. Every highlight you create is automatically saved to your local device using IndexedDB, ensuring your data remains private and accessible even without an internet connection. The extension seamlessly integrates into your browsing experience, allowing you to highlight text with a simple selection and click. You can organize your highlights into custom folders, making it effortless to categorize information by project, topic, or any system that works for you. Whether you're conducting research, studying for exams, or simply collecting interesting ideas, Amber keeps everything neatly organized and instantly searchable.

AI-Powered Summarization & Translation

Leveraging the cutting-edge Gemini Nano APIs built directly into Chrome, Amber brings advanced AI capabilities to your fingertips without requiring external API keys or internet connectivity for processing. The summarization feature intelligently condenses lengthy articles, research papers, or documentation into concise, digestible summaries, saving you valuable time while ensuring you grasp the core concepts. This is particularly useful when you're reviewing multiple sources or need to quickly determine if a document is relevant to your needs. The translation feature breaks down language barriers instantly, allowing you to translate highlighted text into your preferred language with a single click. Both features work offline once the AI model is loaded, making Amber an indispensable tool for multilingual researchers, students, and professionals who need to process information quickly and efficiently across different languages and content volumes.

Flashcards & Active Learning

Transform your passive reading into active learning with Amber's integrated flashcard system. Any highlight you save can be instantly converted into a flashcard, complete with the original text on the front and your personal notes or key takeaways on the back. This feature is perfect for memorizing important definitions, historical dates, foreign language vocabulary, programming syntax, or any information that requires retention through spaced repetition. The flashcard interface allows you to review your saved content in a focused, distraction-free environment, helping reinforce your memory through active recall. You can create dedicated flashcard sets organized by topic or folder, making it easy to study specific subjects when needed. Amber automatically tracks which keywords and concepts you've saved, helping you identify patterns in your learning and ensuring that important information doesn't get lost in the endless scroll of web content. Whether you're preparing for certification exams, learning a new skill, or building expertise in your field, Amber's flashcard system turns your browsing research into lasting knowledge.
